Supermarket Sales Analysis for better Inventory Optimization

Project Overview

The project is based on Data Visualization for analyzing the sales of a particular Supermarket using the dataset which was available on a website. This project will help in optimizing the inventory based on various factors considered while working on the available dataset, such as change in demand of various items with change in climate and so on. Optimizing the inventory of the Supermarket based on the analysis performed in this project will definetely help in increasing the profit margin.

Stepping Up

It is crucial for an organization today to understand its inventory to achieve both efficient and fast operations, that too, at an affordable cost. An effective management of inventory helps in reducing costs which further keeps accounts and finances in check. From a customer’s point of view, it helps you to provide better customer services through fast delivery and low shipping charges, hence, meeting customer expectations. Sales forecasting is a technique to predict sales based on previous history of the business whilst considering several other factors. Better sales forecasting and analysis will help in understanding the most effective way to optimize a Warehouse or Inventory.

Building Tools

Data Visualization is achieved by Plotly's Python and Javascript Libraries. Plotly's Python and Javascript graphing libraries makes interactive and publication-quality graphs. Flask, a micro web-framework of Python has been used as the business layer of the web application. HTML5,CSS3,Javascript and Jquery has been used at the client side. Plotly's JavaScript library also uses D3.js at its core, making it an additional dependency. Plotly is integrated with Flask for the visual representation of the data and further being transferred in the web application as JSON.
